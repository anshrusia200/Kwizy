Rules of the quiz

  1. There are 5 questions in the quiz. Each question has 4 options out of which only one is correct

  2. Initial score of every candidate is 0. For every correct question the candidate gets 1 mark. No marking for unanswered question.

  3. The time for the quiz is fixed, i.e. 25 seconds. After the time limit is reached no question can be answered and the quiz is auto submitted.

  4. The candidate can also submit before the time limit of the quiz.

  5. The quiz is designed to test the basic programming knowledge of the candidate and it is expected that the candidate attempts the quiz in a fair way to improve there knowledge.
